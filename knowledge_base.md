### Sheet: FAQS FE driven

#### Entry 1
**Sl No**: 1
**Type of issue**: Unable to switch on camera
**Basic Trouble Shooting steps**:
  1.Check Camera Connection
     1.1 If you're using an external camera, make sure it’s plugged in properly.
     1.2 If it’s a wireless camera, check if it's connected and has enough battery.
  
  2.Allow Camera Access
  Make sure your computer or browser is allowing the interview room to use your camera.
     2.1 For Windows: Go to Settings > Privacy > Camera and make sure the app is allowed to use the camera.
     2.2 For Mac: Go to System Preferences > Security & Privacy > Camera and make sure the app has access.
     2.3 In your browser, click the padlock icon in the address bar and allow camera access.
  
  3. Test Camera Permissions Using Troubleshooter
       3.1 Open your browser and navigate to https://test.dyte.io/. or https://webcamtests.com/
       3.2 Upon accessing the site, it will request permission to use your camera. Click "Allow" when prompted.
       3.3 The tool will automatically test your camera. If the camera is functioning correctly, you should see your video feed.
       3.4 If the test fails, the tool will provide error messages or suggestions to help identify the issue.
  
  4.Close Other Apps Using the Camera
     4.1 Close any other apps (like Zoom, Skype, or Teams) that might be using the camera.
  
  5. Restart the Interview Room 
      5.1 Close the interview room and open it again to reset the connection.
  
  6. Check Camera Drivers (For Windows)
      6.1 Press Win + X → Select Device Manager → Expand Imaging Devices.
      6.2 Right-click the camera → Select Update Driver → Choose Search Automatically for Drivers.
  
  7. Reset Camera Permissions (For Mac)
      7.1 Open Terminal and run: sudo killall VDCAssistant
      7.2 Restart your Mac and try again.
  
  8. Transfer the chat to Development Team
**Unnamed: 3**:
  After all the checks listed - Ask the user/candidate what is error they are facing,
  Share new fresh/latest interview url/link - post that if the user/candidate is facing the issue,  ask for a screenshot of the error 
  then transfer to technical team

#### Entry 3
**Sl No**: 3
**Type of issue**: Not able to find or click on Join Button
**Basic Trouble Shooting steps**:
  1.Check Internet Speed
     1.1 Ensure your internet speed meets the minimum requirements:
           Upload Speed: At least 5 Mbps.
           Download Speed: At least 10 Mbps.
      1.2 Use an online speed test tool to verify your internet speed.
      1.3 After confirming your internet speed, launch the interview room again.
  If this doesn't resolve the issue, move to Step 2.
  
  2. Check and Update Chrome Browser
      2.1 Open Chrome and go to the settings menu.
      2.2 Navigate to Help > About Google Chrome to check for updates.
      2.3 If an update is available, install it and relaunch Chrome.
      2.4 After relaunching, try accessing the interview link again. launch the interview room again.
  If this doesn't resolve the issue, move to Step 3
  
  3. Refresh and Reload the Page
      3.1 Press Ctrl + F5 (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + R (Mac) to force reload.
  
  4. Clear Browser Cache and Cookies
      4.1 Open Chrome → Go to chrome://settings/clearBrowserData.
      4.2 Select Cached images and files, Cookies, and Site data, then click Clear Data.
  
  5. Try Incognito Mode
      5.1 Open Chrome and press Ctrl + Shift + N, then retry the interview link.
      5.2 If it works, disable extensions in normal mode (chrome://extensions).
  
  6. Switch to Microsoft Edge
      6.1 If the issue persists, use Microsoft Edge with the latest version installed.
      6.2 Ensure Edge is updated by going to Settings > About Microsoft Edge and install any available updates.
      6.3 Open the interview link in Edge to proceed.
  
  7. Check VPN and Firewall Settings
      7.1 If using a corporate VPN, try disconnecting and reloading the page.
      7.2 Temporarily disable the firewall (Windows: Control Panel > Windows Defender Firewall > Turn Off).
  
  8. Flush DNS Cache (For Windows)
      8.1 Open Command Prompt (Admin) and run: ipconfig /flushdns
      8.2 Restart your browser and try again.

#### Entry 5
**Sl No**: 5
**Type of issue**: Panel/Candidate not able to hear the voice
**Basic Trouble Shooting steps**:
  1. Check Volume Settings:
     1.1 Make sure the volume on the PC is turned up.
     1.2 Check the volume icon in the taskbar and ensure it’s not muted.
     1.3 Check if your speakers or headphones are plugged in properly.
  
  2. Check Audio Output Device:
     2.1 Right-click the volume icon in the taskbar and select Open Sound settings.
     2.2 Under the Output section, make sure the correct device (like Speakers or Headphones) is selected.  
  
  3. Check Microphone and Audio Device Connection:
    3.1 Ensure that the microphone or headphones are connected properly (i.e., both the headphones and microphone cables are in the correct ports on the computer).
    3.2 If you're using Bluetooth headphones, ensure they are connected properly and not disconnected.
  
  4. Test with Another Application:
    4.1 Try testing the microphone and speakers/headphones with another app, like the Voice Recorder or Play a video.
  
  5.Test Microphone Input:
    5.1 In Windows, go to Sound settings, under Input, check if the microphone is picking up sound (the bar should move when they speak).
    5.2 Ensure that the microphone is not muted.
         
  6. Check for Mute:
    6.1 Ensure that neither the panel nor the candidate has accidentally muted their microphone or speakers (both in the app and on the physical device).
  
  7.Try Restarting the interview room or Computer:
     7.1 Sometimes, closing and restarting the computer can fix the problem.
  
  8.Test Speaker Output Using Troubleshooter
     8.1 Visit https://test.dyte.io/
     8.2 The site will request access to your speakers. Click "Allow" if prompted.
     8.3 The tool will play a test sound through your speakers. If you can hear the sound, your speakers are functioning correctly.
     8.4 If you do not hear the test sound, the tool may provide error messages or suggestions to assist in diagnosing the issue.
  
  9. Transfer the chat to Development Team
